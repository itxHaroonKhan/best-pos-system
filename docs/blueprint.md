# **App Name**: NexusSales

## Core Features:

- Sales Transaction Processing: Efficiently process sales with barcode scanning, apply discounts, and manage diverse payment methods. 
- Product Inventory Management: Track stock levels, manage product SKUs, and receive automated low-stock alerts.
- Basic Customer Records: Maintain essential customer profiles and easily view their complete purchase history.
- Sales & Financial Reporting: Generate key financial reports, including daily sales summaries and profit/loss statements, from the MySQL database.
- User Role Management & Authentication: Secure user authentication with distinct roles for 'Admin' and 'Cashier', providing appropriate access levels.
- POS Hardware Integration: Seamless integration with barcode scanners for product lookup and thermal printers (ESC/POS) for receipt generation.
- AI-Powered Sales Insights: Utilize an AI tool to analyze and summarize daily sales data, providing concise, actionable insights in natural language for decision-making.

## Style Guidelines:

- Light color scheme emphasizing clarity and professionalism. Primary color: a clean, muted blue-green (#1F7C99) to convey trust and efficiency. Background color: a heavily desaturated, light blue-green (#F0F6F7) for visual comfort. Accent color: a vibrant green (#38DB97) for highlighting key actions and information.
- Headline and body text font: 'Inter', a neutral sans-serif chosen for its high readability and modern, objective aesthetic, suitable for data-intensive applications.
- Utilize clear, functional, and easily recognizable line icons to denote actions and navigation, ensuring an intuitive user experience for retail operations.
- A well-structured, multi-panel layout optimizing screen space for data entry and display. The design prioritizes quick access to sales, inventory, and reporting functions for operational efficiency.
- Subtle and purposeful UI animations, such as smooth transitions for page loads and instant feedback for transactional processes, to enhance user interaction without creating distractions.
